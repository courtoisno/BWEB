Bradley Scott Fletcher is a Creative and Art Director from the UK currently living in San Francisco and working at Goodby, Silverstein & Partners. He’s worked in Advertising for 4+ years concepting and creating works for clients including PlayStation, ASICS, DHL, United Colors of Benetton, Audi, Hewlett Packard, Qatar Airways, UNICEF, Replay Jeans, Heineken, Comcast/Xfinity, Doritos.

His main focus lies within the traditional medias of film and print as well as in his own personal photographic and design projects. Feel free to check out some of the work below.
